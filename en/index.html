Glory to the Father and to the Son and to the Holy Spirit. As it was in the beginning, now and forever. Amen

Jesus, meek and humble of heart, make my heart like yours

Come Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of your faithful and kindle in them the fire of your love. Send your Spirit and everything will be created and you will renew the face of the earth.
Let us pray: O God, who instructed the hearts of your faithful, with the light of the Holy Spirit, grant that we may rightly appreciate all things according to the same Spirit and enjoy his consolation. By Christ Our Lord. Amen

Hail, Queen, Mother of mercy, life, sweetness and our hope, save! We cry out to you, the banished children of Eve; to you we sigh, groaning and weeping in this vale of tears. Come, then, our lawyer, turn those merciful eyes of yours to us; and after this exile, show us Jesus, blessed fruit of thy womb, O merciful, O pious, O sweet ever Virgin Mary. Pray for us, holy Mother of God.
That we may be worthy of the promises of Christ.

Saint Michael Archangel, defend us in combat, be our refuge against the evil and snares of the devil. God ordain him, we urgently beseech him, and you, prince of the heavenly militia, by divine virtue, cast Satan and all evil spirits into hell, who roam the world to lose souls. Amen
